Welcome!

Doggy Tours Toronto is an independent dogwalking and boarding business, located in downtown Toronto. Owner, operator, and chief dogwalker, Andrew Vey, is committed to providing you and your pet with the best possible care.

We offer private and group walks, and are available for nights, weekends, and some holidays (subject to availability). Short-term boarding is available upon request. Visit our Services page for more information.
